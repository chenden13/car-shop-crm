# Airbnb Design System (Inspiration)

Warm, welcoming, and high-trust. Friendly rounded corners and a vibrant coral identity.

## Color Palette

### Brand
- **Rausch (Main)**: `#ff385c` (Primary CTA, Active elements)
- **Deep Rausch**: `#e00b41` (Pressed states)
- **Luxe Purple**: `#460479` (Premium sections)
- **Plus Magenta**: `#92174d` (Special status)

### Text & Neutral
- **Near Black**: `#222222` (Primary text)
- **Secondary**: `#6a6a6a` (Secondary info)
- **Border**: `#ebebeb` (Light borders)
- **Surface**: `#f7f7f7` (Background variation)

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: 'Nunito Sans', 'Inter', -apple-system, sans-serif
- **Weight**: 400 (Regular), 600 (Semi-bold), 700 (Bold)

## Components

### Buttons
- **Style**: Soft rounded corners (`8px`) or fully circular.
- **Primary**: Background: Rausch, Color: White, Shadow on hover.

### Cards & Containers
- **Border Radius**: `20px` to `32px` (Soft and organic).
- **Shadows**:
  - `card`: `rgba(0,0,0,0.02) 0px 0px 0px 1px, rgba(0,0,0,0.04) 0px 2px 6px, rgba(0,0,0,0.1) 0px 4px 8px`
  - `hover`: `rgba(0,0,0,0.08) 0px 4px 12px`

## Interactions
- **Micro-animations**: Subtle scale-up on hover for cards.
- **Atmosphere**: High "white space" usage, very clean and accessible.
